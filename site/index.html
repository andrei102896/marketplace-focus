Does it work only on Marketplace pages?

The extension runs on facebook.com and targets Reels surfaces so Marketplace sessions stay cleaner.

Can I temporarily disable blocking?

Yes. Use the popup toggle or pause button. You can also enforce extra friction through confirmations and hard lock.

Will schedule override manual toggle?

When schedule is enabled, active status follows your selected days and time windows automatically.
